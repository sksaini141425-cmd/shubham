# Project Vision: Automated Trading Bot

## Core Objective
To build a fully automated trading system powered by AI and algorithmic strategies. The ultimate goal is to allow the user to "sit back and let the AI trade for me" while maintaining a high probability of profit.

## Execution Phases

### Phase 1: Paper Trading (Simulated)
Before risking any real capital, the bot will be strictly tested in a **paper trading** environment.
- **Goal**: Forward-test the trading strategies in real-time market conditions with simulated money.
- **Metric for Success**: Achieving a consistent, high percentage of profitable trades and a positive expected value over a significant sample size.

### Phase 2: Live Trading
Once Phase 1 proves successful and reliable, the bot will transition to live trading with real funds.

## Key Principles
- **Exchange Focus**: strictly Binance Futures.
- **Directional Trading**: The bot must trade both Long and Short sides of the market equally based on high-probability setups.
- **High Leverage & Ironclad Risk Management**: The strategy will utilize high leverage (up to 150x) to maximize capital efficiency, demanding the strictest risk management parameters. Capital preservation is paramount, and there will be "no foolishness" with oversized, unhedged losses.
- **Profit Probability**: Aim for a high win rate with reasonable, compounded profit over time.
- **Data-Driven**: All trades will be based on backtested models or real-time quantitative analysis, removing emotion from trading.
- **Automation**: The system should run completely autonomously, requiring minimal intervention.
