## Trading with a $3 Account
- **The $3 Reality Check (09-Mar-2026):** We attempted a 1-minute BTCUSDT scalp (LONG at 67,380) with a $3 account at 50x leverage. The price dropped to 67,308 almost instantly. 
- **The Lesson:** With only a $3 margin balance, there is **zero room for drawdown**. A tiny $70 move on Bitcoin's price (which happens constantly on the 1m chart) will immediately hit the stop loss or liquidate the account before the trade has time to breathe.
- **Rule going forward:** A $3 account CANNOT safely micro-scalp the 1-minute chart. The account will eventually need to trade higher-conviction setups only, or the balance needs to be increased to survive normal market noise.
